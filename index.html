Our Project

Our project aims to address the challenge of facilitating meaningful connections among college students based on their academic interests and potential collaboration opportunities.

We propose the development of a student community website that offers a user-friendly platform for students to create profiles, highlighting their names, academic degrees, and areas of interest.

Our platform will include interactive networking features such as chat forums, discussion boards, and tools for fostering engagement and facilitating organic connections among students with similar academic interests.

By providing these features, our goal is to empower students to collaborate, share knowledge, and explore opportunities for academic growth and networking.
