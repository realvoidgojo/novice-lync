Frequently Asked Questions

You can connect with other students by visiting the Connect page in your dashboard. There you can search for students based on skills, interests, or courses. Once you find someone you'd like to connect with, click the "Connect" button on their profile.

To create a team, go to the Teams page and click "Create New Team". Fill in the required information and invite members. To join a team, you can either accept an invitation or browse available teams in the "Explore Teams" tab.

Your progress is automatically tracked as you complete tasks, participate in projects, and develop skills. Visit the Progress page to see detailed analytics about your activities, skills improvement, and recent achievements.

You can update your profile information by navigating to the User Profile page. Click the "Edit Profile" button to make changes to your personal information, skills, interests, and educational background.
